# Sin Cos Tan

![](/images/2019-08-03-08-32-06.png)

$$ 
sin\theta = \frac{oppsite}{hypotenuse} 
$$

$$ 
cos\theta = \frac{adjacent}{hypotenuse}
$$

$$ 
tan\theta = \frac{oppsite}{adjacent}
$$
